How it works

Example: Mission to Mars

One day = one story with a beginning, middle, and finale. Kids are the protagonists.

1

We just landed on the Red Planet and meet our new crew.

Ice-breaker games, creating our team name, flag, and fun space nicknames.

2

Time to set up our first Martian base.

Handcraft workshop: kids design and build models of shelters and stations from creative materials.

3

On this strange planet we must find something to eat.

Cooking workshop: making space-treats and "Martian sweets" from natural ingredients.

4

We head out to discover mysterious places around our colony.

Creative quest with puzzles and team challenges to unlock "secrets" of the planet.

5

Strange signs show Mars might not be empty‚Ä¶ maybe it's alive.

Art workshop: kids draw, sculpt, or craft the hidden "spirit of the planet."

6

In the end we gather to decide how to live together with this living planet.

Group storytelling & role-play: children co-create the ending of the adventure and share what they built and learned.

Other themes rotate weekly: Underwater City, Jungle Expedition, Medieval Castle, Arctic Rescue, Time Travelers, Secret Agency.
